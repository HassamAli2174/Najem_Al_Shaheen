About the Workshop

Hands-on repair, not guesswork.

Najem Al Shaheen Repairing of Equipment works on the machines that keep facilities, sites and fleets running — rewinding motors and alternators, overhauling generators, repairing PLCs and control panels, and maintaining the chillers and AC systems that can't afford downtime.

Beyond electrical repair, the shop handles fabrication and mechanical engineering work: lathe, milling, drilling, grinding, hydraulic press, and welding — plus full overhaul and maintenance of cranes, heavy trucks, concrete mixers and industrial equipment.
